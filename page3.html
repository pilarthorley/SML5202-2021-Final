El Pretérito/Le Passé Composé AND El Imperfecto/L'imparfait

Spanish Preterite Tense- Regular verb endings

Remove the endings of the verb to leave the stem and add the correct ending:

Personal Pronoun AR ER and IR
I/Yo é í
You/Tú aste iste
he/she/it/él, ella, usted ó ió
We/Nosotros amos imos
You (plural)/Vosotros asteis isteis
They/ellos, ellas, ustedes aron ieron

Spanish Preterite Tense - Most common irregular verbs

These verbs are VERY irregular and frequently used so must be learnt off by heart. Careful! Ser and Ir are the same in the preterite tense:

Personal Pronoun Ser (to be) and Ir (to go) Dar (to give) Ver (to see)
I/Yo fui di vi
You/Tú fuiste diste viste
He,She,it/él, ella, usted fue dio vio
We/Nosotros fuimos dimos vimos
You (plural)/Vosotros fuisteis disteis visteis
Them/ellos, ellas, ustedes fueron dieron vieron
