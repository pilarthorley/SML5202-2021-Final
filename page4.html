El Futuro Simple/Le Futur Proche AND El Futuro Informal/Le Futur Simple

The Simple Future / El Futuro Simple

Spanish Simple Future Tense - Regular verb endings

For all verbs (AR/ER/IR), the endings are the same. Easy peasy!

Personal Pronoun Ending
I/Yo é
You/Tú ás
he/she/it/él, ella, usted á
We/Nosotros emos
You (plural)/Vosotros éis
They/ellos, ellas, ustedes án

Spanish Simple Future Tense - Irregular verb endings

These verbs have irregular stems but the endings stay the same:

Verb Verb after stem change Example
Tener Tendr- Nosotros tendremos
Poner Pondr- Yo pondré
Valer Valdr- Vosotros valdréis
Salir Saldr- Ellos saldrán
Venir Vendr- Ella vendrá

The Informal Future / El Futuro Informal

Spanish Informal Future Tense Formula

This future tense is formed by using a combination of the verb ir, the preposition a, and an infinitive:

Personal Pronoun Ir conjugation Example
I/Yo voy Yo voy a viajar por España
You/Tú vas Tú vas a cantar
He,She,it/El,Ella,Usted va Usted va a ser famoso
We/Nosotros vamos Nosotros vamos a cenar a las nueve
You (plural)/Vosotros Vais Vosotros vais a jugar al fútbol esta tarde
Them/Ellos,Ellas Van Ellos van a dormir en el primer piso.

The French Near Future/ Le Futur Proche

Le Future Proche Formula

To conjugate the futur proche, we use the present tense of the verb aller as an auxiliary verb, followed by the infinitive of the main verb:

Personal Pronoun Aller in present tense Example
I/Je je vais je vais aimer
You/Tu tu vas tu vas finir
He,She,It/Il,Elle,On il/elle/on va il/elle/on va vendre
We/Nous nous allons nous allons manger
You (plural)/Vous vous allez vous allez tomber
They/Ils,Elles ils/elles vont ils/elles vont courir

The Simple Future/ Le Futur Simple

Le Future Simple- Regular verbs

To conjugate the futur simple, we take the infinitive of the verb and add the following endings (for -re verbs, we remove the final e). The endings are the same for every verb ending:

Personal Pronoun Ending Example
I/Je -ai j’aimerai
You/Tu -as tu aimeras
He,She,It/Il,Elle,On -a il/elle/on finira
We/Nous -ons nous finirons
You (plural)/Vous -ez vous vendrez
They/Ils,Elles -ont ils/elles vendront

Le Future Simple- Irregular 'avoir' and 'être'

The verbs avoir and être are irregular in the futur simple, here are their conjugations:

Personal Pronoun Avoir Être
I/Je j’aurai je serai
You/Tu tu auras tu seras
He,She,It/Il,Elle,On il/elle/on aura il/elle/on sera
We/Nous nous aurons nous serons
You (plural)/Vous vous aurez vous serez
They/Ils,Elles ils/elles auront ils/elles seront
