Spanish Present Tense Verb Table - Regular Verbs

Take away the ending of the verb to leave the root e.g. hablar= habl and then add the required ending:

AR ER IR
I/yo o o o
YOU/tu as es es
HE/SHE/IT/él/ella/usted a e e
WE/nosotros/as amos emos imos
YOU(PLURAL)/vosotros/as áis éis ís
THEY/ellos/ellas/ustedes an en en

Spanish Present Tense Verb Table - Irregular verbs

Some verbs change their stem when they are conjugated into the present tense, here are the most common irregular verbs:

Infinitive verb Irregular form (in 'yo')
Caer Caigo
Conocer Conozco
Dar Doy
Hacer Hago
Poner Pongo
Saber Sé
Salir Salgo
Traducir Traduzco
Traer Traigo
Ver Veo

French Present Tense Verb Table - Regular verbs

Remove the ending of the verb e.g. Arriver= arriv and add the following endings:

Infinitive verb ER IR RE
I/Je e is s
You/Tu es is s
He/She/It/Il/Elle/On e it t
We/Nous ons issons ons
You (plural)/Vous ez issez ez
They/Ils/Elles ent issent ent
